Frequently Asked Questions

  • What is MuSNet?

    MuSNet is an open source project whose mission is to create a little ecosystem for Networked Music Performances.

  • What is a Networked Music Performance?

    It can be considered as Networked Music Performance whatever interaction between two or more musicians that takes place in almost real time and by the utilization of a device network.
